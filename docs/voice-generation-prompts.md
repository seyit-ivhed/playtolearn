# Voice Generation Prompts

This document contains the Voice Design prompts for the companions in Math Quest Adventures, formatted for ElevenLabs. Each companion has 4 distinct prompts representing different stages of their evolution. 

As per the game's story, companions grow up through their journey:
- Stage 1 (Evo 0): 8 years old
- Stage 2 (Evo 1): 12 years old
- Stage 3 (Evo 2): 15 years old
- Stage 4 (Evo 3): 18 years old

The preview text for each stage combines their in-game phrases (including success, failure, and level-up) with extra context to give the AI enough length to form a stable voice profile.

---

## 1. Amara — The Jungle Ranger
**Theme:** South American Jungle / Ruins  
**Ability Fantasy:** Precision Shot (Damage)  

### Evolution Stage 1 (Novice / Age 8)
* **Prompt:** Perfect audio quality. An 8-year-old young girl with a thick South American Spanish accent. Her tone is highly energetic, eager, and bright. She speaks at a very fast pace, sounding like an excited and brave child adventurer.
* **Preview Text:** "Oh, I see them! ¡Vayamos muy rápido ahora! We have to move now before they get away! I got you now! That is a target hit! You are too slow! Let's try that again! I am getting taller!"
* **Guidance Scale:** 30%

### Evolution Stage 2 (Swift Hunter / Age 12)
* **Prompt:** Perfect audio quality. A 12-year-old pre-teen girl with a clear South American Spanish accent. Her voice is athletic and focused, starting to show confidence. She speaks quickly but more deliberately than a child, like a capable young hunter.
* **Preview Text:** "I have the target in my sights. ¡Tengo puntería muy certera! There is nowhere to hide in this jungle! Right in the bullseye! That was a direct hit! I have an eagle eye! Almost had it, retry! I feel much stronger!"
* **Guidance Scale:** 30%

### Evolution Stage 3 (Sharpshooter / Age 15)
* **Prompt:** Perfect audio quality. A 15-year-old teenage girl with a deliberate South American Spanish accent. Her tone is sharp, intense, and slightly lower-pitched. She speaks at a measured pace, exuding the focus of a growing sharpshooter.
* **Preview Text:** "Breathe in, breathe out. ¡Mi flecha es letal! Every arrow finds its mark. My aim is perfect! There is no escape! That was a flawless shot! Stay focused, try again! I am growing wiser!"
* **Guidance Scale:** 35%

### Evolution Stage 4 (Master Ranger / Age 18)
* **Prompt:** Perfect audio quality. An 18-year-old young woman with a commanding South American Spanish accent. Her voice is clear, serious, and deeply confident. The pacing is deliberate and measured, as if she is savoring each word. Her voice sounds absolutely lethal and fully matured. There is also a magical, unnatural tone to it.
* **Preview Text:** "The jungle obeys my command now. ¡Soy la reina de la selva! I never miss my prey. The jungle obeys me! I never miss prey! Nature guides my arrow! Focus, the hunt continues! I have fully matured!"
* **Guidance Scale:** 35%
