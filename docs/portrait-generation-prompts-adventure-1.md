# Monster Portrait Prompts: Adventure 1

These prompts are designed for generating child-friendly, yet adversarial monster portraits for "The Oasis Quest". They focus on mischievous, grumpy, or territorial personalities so they feel like adversaries players should battle.

## Dune Scorpion (Encounter 1_1)
```
Portrait of a Dune Scorpion, territorial and snappy, small creature with sturdy rounded pinchers, narrowed expressive eyes, and a tough golden sandy shell. Stubby legs and a curled tail held in a defensive, ready-to-snap posture. Looking grumpy and guarding its sand dune. Stylized 3D digital illustration, child-friendly, bright vibrant colors, soft lighting. Safe for children.
```

## Sand Spirit (Encounter 1_2)
```
Portrait of a Sand Spirit, mischievous and blocking the path, floating creature made of swirling dark-golden sand with sharp-edged cloud shapes. Big glowing restless blue eyes and a smirk showing a hint of a swirling mouth. Looking like it's about to play a mean trick. Stylized 3D digital illustration, child-friendly, bright vibrant colors, soft glow lighting. Safe for children.
```
