# Art Generation Prompts

## Adventure 1 Map Background

**Prompt:**
A vertical scrolling fantasy map background on ancient worn parchment paper. The map depicts a journey through a dangerous desert, flowing from top to bottom without any text, labels, or grid lines. Edges and corners should fade to black with black background.

At the top, the terrain consists of rolling sand dunes and small scattered rocks, evoking a dry, scorpion-infested wasteland.

Moving downwards, the landscape becomes more rugged with jagged rocky outcrops and canyon walls, hinting at hidden ambushes. Soft wind patterns and swirling sand textures appear in the lower-middle section, representing ethereal spirits.

At the very bottom, the map opens into a vast, ominous crater-like arena surrounded by towering sand formations, the lair of a massive colossus.

The style is hand-drawn ink with sepia and brown tones, weathered paper texture, burnt edges, and detailed cartographic linework. High contrast, atmospheric shading, no markers, no paths, no UI elements.

Try to make it as long as possible.
