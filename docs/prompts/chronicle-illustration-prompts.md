# Chronicles Illustration Prompts

This document provides image generation prompts for the **Chronicles** section (Chapter Illustrations) for the first two adventures. These illustrations are designed to be scenic storybook-style art that sets the atmosphere for the adventure.

---

## Art Style Guidelines

To maintain consistency with the *Math Quest Adventures* aesthetic, use the following style keywords for all Chapter Illustrations:

- **Art Style:** `Hand-painted digital illustration`, `stylized fantasy art`, `rich painterly textures`, `storybook illustration style`.
- **Lighting:** `Dramatic lighting`, `atmospheric shading`, `cinematic composition`.
- **Colors:** `Warm vibrant colors`, `regal jewel tones`.
- **Target:** `Google Nano Banana` (or other high-quality SDXL-based generators).
- **Aspect Ratio:** `16:9` or `3:2` (recommended for the `ChronicleBook` UI).

## Adventure 2: The Ruins of Ashur

### Concept
A breathtaking view of the subterranean city, filled with ancient wonder and bandit encampments.

### Primary Prompt
> A breathtaking storybook illustration of the vast subterranean city of Ashur. Grand ancient Assyrian ruins with towering pillars and crumbling stone palaces carved into the cave walls. The cavern is illuminated by clusters of glowing blue crystals and flickering campfires from a bandit camp nestled among the debris. Ethereal teal and purple lighting, mysterious atmosphere. Hand-painted digital illustration, stylized fantasy art, rich painterly textures, cinematic composition.

### Variation (Action/Tension)

Towering Assyrian statues with wings stand guard at a crumbling temple entrance. Vibrant teal crystal clusters provide a magical glow against the deep purple cavern shadows. Hand-painted digital illustration, rich painterly textures, high quality.

## Tips for Best Results
1. **Aspect Ratio:** Ensure you specify `--ar 16:9` if the generator supports parameters.
2. **Adversarial Balance:** For Chronicles, keep the focus on the **journey and discovery** rather than a specific battle.
3. **Consistency:** Use "Hand-painted digital illustration" as the leading style keyword to ensure it blends well with character portraits.
