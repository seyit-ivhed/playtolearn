## Amara: Precision Shot

### Magical Hunter's Arrow
Used as the projectile for Amara's "Precision Shot" special ability.

**Prompt:**
> A single, legendary fantasy arrow shown from a profile side-view, perfectly horizontal. The arrow shaft is made of dark, polished mahogany wood with delicate golden filigree carvings. The fletching consists of elegant, sharp-cut feathers in vibrant emerald green and deep jungle blue. The arrowhead is forged from translucent magical crystal, glowing with an intense, focused golden light at its tip. Stylized high-fantasy RPG asset, hand-painted digital illustration style, rich painterly textures, sharp and precise. Isolated on a solid black background. 2:1 aspect ratio, high resolution.
